About Aranya Earthcraft

Aranya Earthcraft is dedicated to bring home the elegance of nature and delicacy of art.

Here, with art, we try to follow the aesthetics of nature. A leaf, a dancing body, a blooming lotus, there is a limitless energy that navigates through the nature. We aspire to embody this elegance in our wide range of products.

An initiative founded by Preeti Gupta and Dr. Vivek Prasad, is an effort to design and create environment-friendly designer products that can enliven your home.

At Aranya Earthcraft our expertise is in home decor products and jewelleries. We make them with the age-old techniques of papier-mache and recyclable waste material. Nothing is used that would harm the nature.

Our products are seamless fusion of art, design and nature. Human forms are another concept that we are developing with a unique approach.

Here you will feel the energising inspiration that flows in the natural world: colour, material, expression, everything is to make you feel inspired and motivated. To feel at home.

The creativity of Aranya Earthcraft team is self-taught. Having an experience of 15 years of working in the field of design and art has helped us to learn and evolve the various forms and techniques of designs. At the core, our belief is ‘the Nature as the source of inspiration for our creativity’.
